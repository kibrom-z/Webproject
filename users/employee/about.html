About the company

Zemen Express Cooperate (ZEC) is one of the prominent long distance bus transportation companies in Ethiopia. It was founded in 2010 to address the nationwide need for public transportation. Zemen Express Cooperate currently provides service to more than 13 cities in Ethiopia. The headquarters is located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia.

At present, the company is rendering service from Addis to Dire Dawa, Harrar, Jimma, Hawassa, and Arbaminch on daily basis. The headquarters, bus terminal and garage of ZEC has been established in Addis Ababa with branch offices in all regional capitals. Buses departing from Addis Ababa to all the regional capitals providing all necessary information and entertainment services to the satisfaction of the passengers are expected to serve as the ambassadors of the region. The company have plans to buy additional thirteen buses in near future to expand its services.

ZEC provides the public transport service to travelers in the country. With their continuous efforts to be leaders and offer a competitive service they aim at satisfying the mobility needs of current and potential users, with a top-quality service and practical information, with competitive costs and contributing to the sustainability of the environment.

In regard to customer orientation and improved services, the company works on policies for improved customer service and campaigns to encourage the use of the bus. And also, continuous improvement of services, as regards the number of routes offered, journey times, punctuality and regularity, safety and comfort.
