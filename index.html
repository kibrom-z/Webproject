Our mission

“We provide the public transport service to travelers in the country. With our continuous efforts to be leaders and offer a competitive service we aim at satisfying the mobility needs of current and potential users, with a top quality service and practical information, with competitive costs and contributing to the sustainability of our environment.”

In regard to customer orientation and improved services, we work on policies for improved customer service and campaigns to encourage the use of the bus. And also, continuous improvement of services, as regards the number of routes offered, journey times, punctuality and regularity, safety and comfort.
